## Data Science Lab Project
Digital Financial Literacy: A Multidimensional Posetic Analysis of the 2023 Italian Population

---

### :bar_chart: Project Description
This project, developed as part of the **Data Science Lab** course within the MSc in Data Science, investigates **Digital Financial Literacy (DFL)** in Italy using microdata from the 2023 Bank of Italy survey (IACOFI).  
The analysis is based on the **OECD/INFE** framework and focuses on modelling digital financial literacy as a **multidimensional phenomenon** composed of knowledge, behaviour, and attitude. 

---

### :dart: Objectives
- Construct **multidimensional profiles** of digital financial literacy, analyse their relations and distribution across the Italian population through a **POSET framework**
- Identify main **structural bottlenecks** in digital financial literacy
- Analyse potential **mediating factors** between the three main indicators 
- Investigate differences in digital financial skills across **socio-economic groups**
