### :bar_chart: Project Description
This project, developed as part of the **Data Visualization** course within the MSc in Data Science, focuses on analysing and visualizing the **World Happiness Report data (2015-2024)** from Kaggle.  
The goal is to tell **journalistic stories** through the data.

---

### :dart: Objectives
- Analyse the evolution of **global happiness levels** over the last decade
- Identify the **key drivers** most strongly correlated with overall happiness
- Examine the **European happiness landscape** and its changes over time
- Investigate **regional dynamics and inequalities**
