## Social Issues in Information Society Take-Home Assignment
The Morphology of Uncertainty: Power and Control in the Digital Age

---

### :bar_chart: Project Description
This project, developed as part of the **Social Issues in Information Society** course within the MSc in Data Science and inspired by Luciano Floridi's claim *"Whoever controls uncertainty controls society"*, focuses on how uncertainty has become a central mechanism of power and governance in the Digital Age.

---

### :dart: Objectives
- Examine the concept of **power** and its historical evolution
- Analyze **uncertainty** as a strategic resource for governance and control
- Investigate the role of **digital platforms** and **states** in producing and shaping uncertainty
- Discuss **future scenarios** and regulatory approaches
